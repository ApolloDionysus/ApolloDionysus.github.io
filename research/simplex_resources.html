Simplex algorithm from the perspective of SMT solvers.

urgent! It seems I have neglected the SMT solver part of the simplex algorithm used in reluplex. We only use phase one simplex since we are only checking if the equations are satisifiable or unsatisifiable. This is usually followed by phase two simplex where we optimize a cost function.

The DPLL(T) and splitting on demand mechanisms are used to make decisions. So while simplex checks satisifiablity, it does not explain how we choose to pivot, update, or assign. That is deligated by the DPLL(T). It seems to me that the DPLL is what we are should try to optimize in hardware, then, rather than the simplex algorithm.
